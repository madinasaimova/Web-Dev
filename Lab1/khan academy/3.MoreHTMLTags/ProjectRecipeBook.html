Pasta with Tomato Sauce

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 300g
Tomato Sauce 2 cups
Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Salt To taste

Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a pan, heat olive oil, add minced garlic, and saut√© until golden.

Step 3: Pour in the tomato sauce, add salt to taste, and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 4: Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce until well-coated.
